# Luna Vault Vision

Luna Vault is brand infrastructure and brand management software. It is where brands live after they are designed.

The product connects brand systemisation, professional handover, asset documentation, ongoing brand management, brand and sub-brand governance, version certainty, team access, and controlled sharing with clients and vendors.

## The problem

Brand design produces more than files. It produces a system of assets, rules, relationships, decisions, and ownership. Conventional folders preserve the files but lose much of that meaning. Over time, teams become uncertain about what is approved, current, relevant, or safe to use.

Luna Vault preserves the system around the files. Its core outcome is confidence: a user should be able to find the correct asset and understand how it belongs to the brand without asking its original designer.

## What Luna Vault is not

Luna Vault is not:

- Google Drive or Dropbox
- a generic file manager
- a logo builder
- Luna Studio
- an AI design generator
- a heavyweight enterprise Digital Asset Management system

Storage supports the product, but storage alone is not the product.

## Initial customers

Luna Vault is initially designed for:

- freelance brand designers
- small design studios
- branding agencies
- startups
- small internal marketing teams

## Market entry and long-term category

The initial wedge is **professional brand handover**: helping a designer deliver a complete, structured, client-ready brand system rather than a folder of exports.

The long-term category is **brand infrastructure and brand operations**: the ongoing system through which organisations govern, understand, distribute, and evolve their brands.
